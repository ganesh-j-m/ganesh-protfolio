Carrer katta

When I was in my first year, our college introduced an initiative called “Career Katta.”
I participated in it, and I was selected as the Information & Communication Minister of
our college for one year.Through this platform, we organized several activities and events
that contributed to our overall personal and professional development. This experience
sparked my interest in such initiatives and alongside my studies,
I continued to take part in them.

    1. I developed **stage confidence and courage to speak in front of others.
    2. I got an **opportunity to work as a minister in college.
    3. I had **interaction, discussion, and guidance from successful entrepreneurs** who run their own companies in India and abroad.
